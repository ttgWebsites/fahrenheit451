Interesting Details

  • Though a common belief is that this novel is about censorship, Bradbury himself has said that he instead was writing about how our interest in literature is being affected by television.
  • The novel that is now read initially began as a short story titled 'Bright Phoenix', this short story was then expanded into another short story 'The Fireman', and finally into Fahrenheit 451.
  • Bradbury wrote the novel entirely in the basement of a library, he also had to pay to use a typewriter to write the novel. However it turned out to be a small price to pay after his novel became one of the most successful science fiction novel's ever written.
