Basic Info

  • Title
  • Fahrenheit 451
  • Author
  • Ray Bradbury
  • Published
  • United States, Ballantine Books, 1953
  • Genre
  • Science Fiction, Futuristic
  • Setting
  • The novel is set in an unknown city a few hundred years in the future, following two nuclear wars.
  • Point Of View
  • The novel is told in first person from Montag, the protagonist's point of view.
  • Conflict
  • The main conflict in the novel surrounds how Montag views the society he lives in. In Montag's community everyone seems to follow orders and remain as uneducated as possible, they are forbidden to read books and powerful sedatives are popular drugs. Montag comes to view the lifestyle to which he has been accustom to as a horrible way to live, as he comes to realize that being controlled in the way he and everyone else is not really living anyways. He sets out to change his life, to better understand what it means to be human, and escape the confines of the suppressive dystopia.
