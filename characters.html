Characters

  • Guy Montag
  • The protagonist of the novel and a man of about 30 years old
  • Fireman, yet not in the typical sense of the word, he burns books as all books are banned in his society
  • After 10 years and coming to a realization of what his work entails decides he does not like being a fireman any longer and decides to discontinue his work
  • Mildred Montag
  • Mildred is Guy's wife. She is lazy, spending nearly all her time watching tv
  • She appears very sickly and weak due to her lack of outside stimulus
  • Mildred is very boring and unappealing, she refuses to even hold conversations with her husband
  • She attempts suicide but fails, she seems to be harboring deep pain
  • Clarisse McCellan
  • A young girl of about sixteen, she lives near the Montag's
  • Clarisse is a very odd girl during the time period of the novel as she enjoys such things as the outdoors and also often asks questions
  • Her and her family are outcasts to the rest of society
  • Professor Faber
  • Faber is a former English teacher who still holds a passion for literature even though books have been outlawed
  • He and Guy know have known each other since before the novel even starts
  • Faber is frustrated with himself as he believes he is a coward for not standing up for books before they became banned
  • Captain Beatty
  • Captain Beatty leads Guy's firehouse
  • Though he seems well educated he holds a deep hatred for books which explains why he might be a firefighter
  • He keeps a very close eye on Montag, even appearing to know what Montag is thinking
  • Granger
  • He is the leader of a band of rebels who still read and want to keep books alive during this time
  • Granger is also very intelligent
