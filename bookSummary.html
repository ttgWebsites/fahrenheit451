Book Summary

  • So basically...
  • Fahrenheit 451 tells the story of Guy Montag who lives during a futuristic dystopian society. In the novel Montag serves as a fireman that instead of puts out fires, starts homes that have books inside ablaze. Montag meets a young girl named Clarisse that talks to him and helps change his prospective on life. Montag begins to take an interest in books, however the other firemen soon discover him. Montag eventually is caught and forced to burn his own home down, he escapes to try and start a new life with other men who are also interested in books. The city that Montag lived in is eventually destroyed by war and Montag and other men set out to reestablish a society, except this time a better one where literature is commonplace.
